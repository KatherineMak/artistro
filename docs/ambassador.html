Artistro Ambassador Program

Share

Join

You as an Artistro Ambassador are inspiring creators from around the world. You help us to keep our finger on the pulse of your communities and provide us with invaluable feedback so that we can improve and grow.

Through Artistro Ambassador Program you receive tools and experiences, product to try out, a network of like-minded people, and support from us. Our relationship opens up the possibility to get up to big things together - to make a difference in artistic communities!

You are not in just a partnership, you are an extension to the Artistro brand! You are the face of the brand!

How we work together

Inspiration

Our ambassadors get insider information about the brand, including key brand, messaging, and campaign messaging. We will have genuine, one-on-one conversations about our brand and how to promote it.

Product

Our ambassadors receive Artistro products and get a chance to test new products before its launch on the market.

Promotion

Our ambassadors give new customers a reason to trust Artistro by creating tutorials, artworks, and organizing other activities using our excellent product.

Become an Ambassador

They should be older than 18 years old.

They should be from one of these countries: the United States, the United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, Germany, France, Spain, Italy, or the Netherlands.

They should know the social media tools and assist in content creating, therefore, they should have an established online presence (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok and etc) with a high engagement rate.

They should have a large network and work/have a hobby in a sphere of art-creating/crafting.
